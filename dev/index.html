FAQ
Who can attend?
The hackathon is open to all college and graduate students who can make the trip. A school email is required for registration.
When and where is it taking place?
February 27-28th, Locatelli Activity Center, Santa Clara University from 10am-10am.
I've never been to a Hackathon, is that okay?
Absolutely! There are lots of beginners at every hackathon. Hackathons are a great way to develop your skills and see what you can accomplish.
Do I have to develop for the prompts?
You do not. While development for the Looma project is encouraged, you are free to develop applications with some other humanitarian issue in mind, and will still be considered for the prizes.
What is the team size?
Teams must be comprised of four or less people.
I don't have a team...
That's perfectly fine! We will help link you up with other students looking for teammates, and you can also fly solo if you'd prefer.
I have another question...
No problem! Contact Robbie at raldrich@scu.edu and he'll be happy to clear things up.
